About

Giant Yorkshire Puddings, Filled to the Brim

We serve classic British roast flavours in a fun, handheld way: crisp Yorkshire puddings turned into hearty bowls and wraps, loaded with slow-cooked meat, roast potatoes, veggies and proper sauces. Everything is prepared with care, using Halal ingredients.
